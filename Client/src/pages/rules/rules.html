Welcome to Stockholm Light Pursuit!
This is a guide for you tourists that like walking around in Stockholm. If you want to learn more about Stockholm look after purple coloured street lights.

There is a map where you can see position of the street lights that are containing information about Stockholm.

All you need to do is to find a street light that is purple, go to it and connect to it via Bluetooth on your phone. The information then will be shown on a display.

You can visit as many street lights as you want and then you can share your daily routes on Facebook.
Good luck!
