Welcome to Stockholm LightPursuit!

The place for you to be if you want to get a more enjoyable stay in Stockholm. By using this app whilst travelling around here during your stay you will learn much interesting and hopefully fun information about our beautiful capital!

THE BASICS

  • Works through the special purple lightposts posted around the city

  • Learning the culture and history of Stockholm is the purpose of the app, accessing the information is done by being close to one of the lightposts and connecting via bluetooth

  • This is not a very complicated app and the functions are few. To get you started we explain the 3 main functions of the app on the following slides

    •

    LightMap
    This button will take you to the central fucntion of the app which is the map of Stcokholm itself. There you can find everything you need, like where you are, the interesting buildings and museums, and of course the locations of the lightposts.

    Achievments
    Through using this app you can get access to discounts by completing achievments which u can track by pressing this button. The discounts are for attractions that you can visit accross the city.

    Daily route
    After each day of using the app you can check where you walked around during that day, which lightposts u visited and also add attractions to a map. You can then share this with your friends and family back home.
